### Understanding the Shipping Process and Managing Expectations

The "how long does Litbuy take to ship" question is multi-faceted. It's not a single number, but rather a sequence of events:

1.  **Seller to Litbuy Warehouse (Domestic China Shipping):** This is the first leg. Once you place an order with Litbuy, they purchase the item from the original seller. The seller then ships it to Litbuy's consolidation warehouse in China. This usually takes **3-7 days**, but can sometimes stretch to 10-14 days if the seller is slow or the item is made-to-order.
2.  **Warehouse Processing & Consolidation:** Once your item(s) arrive at the Litbuy warehouse, they are weighed, measured, potentially photographed (if you paid for inspection services), and held until all items in your order arrive. If you're combining multiple items, this wait time is dictated by the *last* item to arrive. This can add another **1-5 days**.
3.  **International Shipping (Litbuy Warehouse to Your Door):** This is the longest and most variable part. Litbuy offers various shipping methods, each with different speeds and costs.
    *   **Economy/Standard Shipping (ePacket, China Post, etc.):** This is often the cheapest option and also the slowest, typically taking **15-30 business days (3-6 weeks)**. In some cases, especially during peak seasons or with customs delays, it can extend to **45-60 days**.
    *   **Express Shipping (DHL, FedEx, UPS):** These are significantly faster but also considerably more expensive. You can expect delivery in **5-10 business days**, sometimes even quicker for major destinations. This is the choice for when you absolutely need something faster, but be prepared for the price tag.
    *   **Special Line Shipping:** Some agents offer "special lines" that are tailored to specific countries, often balancing cost and speed. These can fall anywhere in between, perhaps **10-20 business days**.
